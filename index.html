biography

05 May.

2020

In 2020

They year Creatum was born, known before as Gonzo Digital. We started as a purely SMM agency, taking 2020 as a year to build our reputation among the market players and trust from our clients. This year helped us accumulate 7 experts in-house and deliver 14 projects that supported our clients growth.

2021

Starting in 2021, we moved into the office bringing the level of our work and bonding to a whole new level. We grew both internally: 15 experts in-house and externally: opening up for Branding, Performance marketing and Web Development projects.

2022

Start of the year - we are on a roll. Than the War begins, shifting all of our plans from growing to maintaining. Crisis management for clients, shift of ad placements, and unfortunately dispersion of the team around the Globe.

Now

Hurt, not broken. We matured and did our best to help our clients prosper in these times, while maintaining a clear state of mind both for the team members and for clients. We are growing again and are open for new memorable digital projects.
